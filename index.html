Innovation Accounting

Turn sequence

  1. Accounting phase – a measurement card is allocated.
  2. Planning phase – players plan their actions by placing their blocks on the board.
  3. Action phase – one by one, players carry out all their planned actions.

Hiring employees

  • You gain 1 employee each for:
  • First green, yellow and orange tile.
  • 4, 7, and 9 customer tiles overall.
